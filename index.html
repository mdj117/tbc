About Us

We are the official STEM Club of Jayshree Periwal International School.

Mission

The mission of the Bohring club is to cultivate an appreciation for the STEM fields amongst students in high school. It is to integrate STEM fields in a manner that will inspire kids to take up STEM as a part of their daily school routine, be it through academic courses or activities. Moreover, we believe that through Bohring kids’ love for science not only grows tenfold but at the same time, they are able to contribute to society and help solve every day real world problems through the knowledge they gain through STEM. And the whole idea is to not discriminate. No one has to be an ardent lover of physics or biology to be a part. Just share a passion that the rest of us have for the world of science, technology, mathematics and engineering.

Purpose

In a day and age where the greatest innovation and the most ground-breaking research is being done amongst the studies of STEM related subjects, Bohring helps to further that drive. So many kida across the country pursue STEM-related careers without ever actually coming to love them, or finding a passion for them. We decided that this needed to change. In a country where students are often set to pursue STEM against their own wishes, we wanted them to realise that doing so can be fun; that it doesn’t have to feel like a chore. To give an outlet to students to do whatever they please within this vast world.

ScoutMe.in

ScoutMe is a one of a kind, football scouting platform, which will soon upgrade to multi sport disciplines. With the digital media taking over printing press, it's important for multi sport disciplines to upgrade to digital platforms.

With our unique as well as exhaustive system of rating players, a scout can create, edit, organise, and consult the data at any moment, any time and in any medium (computer, tablet or smartphone). This data may be accessed by the technical director or the head scout as and when there arises a need to do so.

ScoutMe is built with the aim to make scouting an easier and a more transparent process. It can be used by clubs to scout players and store data in their database, allowing them to access these records whenever necessary.

ScoutMe aims to help clubs look for raw talent from every corner of India, and help develop them into successful players.

Pluto

Rural Internet of Things.

PLUTO is a device that enables the user to remotely connect the Submersible Pump / Tullu Pump /any other Electrical device to its source of power from any corner of the world by using a phone(Landline /Mobile). It provides the user with accurate information of the Power/Electricity status of the area where it is being used. The principal benefit that the user gains is that with PLUTO, he/she does not have to be physically present to switch ON/OFF the water pump.
